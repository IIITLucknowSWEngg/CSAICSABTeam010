# **Scope of the Decathlon Website Project**

The scope of the Decathlon website project encompasses all aspects necessary to design, develop, and implement a comprehensive e-commerce platform tailored to meet the needs of Decathlon’s diverse user base. This section outlines the boundaries of the project, detailing what is included, as well as what is excluded, to ensure a clear understanding of the deliverables and objectives.

## **In-Scope**

### **User Interface and Experience (UI/UX) Design**

- **Website Layout:** Design and development of a user-friendly and responsive website layout that caters to various devices (desktop, tablet, mobile).
- **Navigation:** Implementation of intuitive navigation structures to facilitate easy browsing and product discovery.
- **Accessibility:** Ensuring the website complies with accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG) to provide an inclusive experience for all users.

### **Product Catalog Management**

- **Product Listings:** Development of functionalities for adding, updating, and managing product information, including descriptions, prices, images, and specifications.
- **Search and Filter:** Implementation of advanced search capabilities and filtering options to help users find products quickly and efficiently.

### **E-commerce Functionality**

- **Shopping Cart:** Design and development of a shopping cart system that allows users to add, modify, and remove items.
- **Checkout Process:** Implementation of a streamlined checkout process, including user authentication, address entry, payment processing, and order confirmation.
- **Payment Integration:** Integration with payment gateways to support various payment methods (credit/debit cards, digital wallets, etc.).

### **User Account Management**

- **Account Creation:** Functionality for users to create and manage accounts, including registration, login/logout, and password recovery.
- **Order History:** Development of features for users to view their past orders and track current shipments.

### **Content Management System (CMS)**

- **Content Updates:** Tools for internal staff to update product information, manage promotions, and post news or blog content.
- **Media Management:** Features for uploading, managing, and displaying multimedia assets (images, videos).

### **Integration with Backend Systems**

- **Inventory Management:** Integration with existing inventory systems to ensure real-time stock updates and accurate product availability.
- **Customer Relationship Management (CRM):** Integration with CRM systems to track customer interactions and support personalized marketing efforts.
- **Analytics:** Integration of analytics tools to monitor site performance, user behavior, and sales metrics.

### **Customer Support**

- **Support Channels:** Implementation of support features, such as live chat, email support, and a help center with FAQs and troubleshooting guides.
- **Returns and Exchanges:** Development of a system to manage product returns, exchanges, and related customer service requests.

### **Compliance and Security**

- **Data Protection:** Ensuring compliance with data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR) and implementing necessary security measures to protect user data.
- **Site Security:** Implementation of security protocols to safeguard against threats and ensure safe transactions.

### **Marketing and Promotions**

- **Promotional Campaigns:** Features for managing and displaying promotional offers, discounts, and seasonal campaigns.
- **Email Marketing Integration:** Integration with email marketing systems to support newsletters, promotional emails, and customer engagement.

# Definitions, Acronyms, and Abbreviations

## Definitions

### **User**

**Definition:** An individual or entity that interacts with the Decathlon website. This includes:

- **Customer:** A person who browses, purchases, or interacts with the website for personal or commercial reasons.
- **Store Manager:** A staff member responsible for managing inventory and store operations within the website.
- **Administrator:** An internal user with access to system settings and user management.
- **Support Agent:** A person responsible for assisting customers with inquiries, returns, and other issues.

### **Product Catalog**

**Definition:** The collection of all products available on the Decathlon website, including details such as descriptions, prices, images, and availability.

### **Content Management System (CMS)**

**Definition:** The software used to manage and update content on the website, including product information, promotional materials, and multimedia.

### **E-commerce Platform**

**Definition:** The underlying system that supports online transactions, including shopping cart functionality, checkout processes, and payment processing.

### **Inventory Management System**

**Definition:** The system used to track and manage stock levels, ensuring that product availability is accurately reflected on the website.

### **Customer Relationship Management (CRM) System**

**Definition:** The system used to manage and analyze customer interactions and data to enhance customer relationships and support personalized marketing.

### **Analytics Tools**

**Definition:** Tools used to collect, analyze, and report on website performance, user behavior, and sales metrics.

### **User Account Management**

**Definition:** The system functionality allowing users to create, manage, and access their accounts, including personal information and order history.

### **Security Measures**

**Definition:** The protocols and technologies implemented to protect the website and user data from unauthorized access, breaches, and other security threats.

### **Compliance and Accessibility Standards**

**Definition:** Legal and regulatory requirements that the website must adhere to, including data protection laws (e.g., GDPR) and accessibility guidelines (e.g., WCAG).

### **Marketing and Promotion Tools**

**Definition:** Systems and features used to manage and execute marketing campaigns, promotions, and customer engagement strategies.

# Characteristics of Users

## 1. Customer

**Definition:** A Customer is an individual who interacts with the Decathlon website to browse, purchase, and engage with products and services.

**Characteristics:**

- **Demographics:** Varies widely including age, gender, location, and income level. The diversity of the customer base necessitates a broad range of product offerings and personalization.
- **Behavior:** Engages in activities such as product search, comparison, and purchase. Typically seeks a user-friendly interface, efficient transaction process, and responsive customer service.
- **Preferences:** Values intuitive navigation, accurate product information, and secure payment options. Prefers personalized recommendations based on browsing history and past purchases.
- **Technical Skills:** Ranges from basic to advanced, influencing their interaction with the website's features. The design must accommodate users with varying levels of digital literacy.
- **Access Needs:** Requires access to product details, pricing, availability, and customer support. May need accessibility features to cater to disabilities.

## 2. Store Manager

**Definition:** A Store Manager is an internal user responsible for overseeing inventory management and ensuring accurate product information on the website.

**Characteristics:**

- **Responsibilities:** Manages inventory levels, updates product information, and monitors stock availability. Ensures that the online catalog reflects current stock accurately.
- **Skills:** Requires proficiency in inventory management systems, understanding of product data, and ability to use content management tools.
- **Access Needs:** Needs access to backend systems for updating inventory, processing returns, and generating sales reports. Requires tools for monitoring stock levels and addressing discrepancies.
- **Technical Proficiency:** Typically has intermediate to advanced technical skills, as they work with specialized software and systems.

## 3. Administrator

**Definition:** An Administrator is an internal user with comprehensive access to the website’s backend systems, responsible for managing site settings and user accounts.

**Characteristics:**

- **Responsibilities:** Configures site settings, manages user roles and permissions, and oversees system integrations. Ensures that all operational aspects of the website function smoothly.
- **Skills:** Advanced technical skills, including knowledge of system configurations, user management, and security protocols. Requires familiarity with various backend systems and tools.
- **Access Needs:** Full access to backend systems, including CMS, e-commerce platform, and analytics tools. Requires capabilities to modify site settings, manage integrations, and oversee overall system performance.
- **Technical Proficiency:** High level of technical expertise to handle complex configurations and troubleshoot issues.

## 4. Support Agent

**Definition:** A Support Agent is an individual responsible for providing assistance and resolving issues for customers interacting with the website.

**Characteristics:**

- **Responsibilities:** Handles customer inquiries, processes returns and exchanges, and resolves complaints. Provides guidance on website usage and troubleshooting.
- **Skills:** Strong communication skills, problem-solving abilities, and a thorough understanding of the website’s features and policies. Needs to use support tools and CRM systems effectively.
- **Access Needs:** Access to customer interaction records, support ticketing systems, and order management tools. Requires capabilities to view customer details and manage support requests.
- **Technical Proficiency:** Moderate to advanced technical skills, including familiarity with support and CRM systems.
